Helpful Tips

For your security, new passwords must meet the following requirements:

  • must be between 8 to 40 characters in length
  • must contain at least one upper case character, on lower case character and one number
  • cannot be a previous used password
  • avoid using special characters
For enhanced security, do not use proper names, words commonly found in the dictionary or repeating sequences of numbers.
